Add PingWA to your home screen

Use it like a native app — no App Store needed. Follow the steps for your device.

iPhone / iPad

  1. 1 Open PingWA in Safari (not Chrome).
  2. 2 Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen.
  3. 3 Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. 4 Tap Add — done!

Android

  1. 1 Open PingWA in Chrome.
  2. 2 Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top-right corner.
  3. 3 Tap Add to Home screen.
  4. 4 Tap Add — done!
